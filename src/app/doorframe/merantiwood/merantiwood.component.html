Origin

Meranti grows throughout all of the major islands of Southeast Asia, mostly in the Philippines, Malaysia, and Indonesia

Advantages

1)Meranti wood has a natural oil content that helps repel water and prevent moisture absorption, reducing the risk of warping, cracking, or swelling. This makes it a reliable choice for outdoor furniture that may be exposed to rain, humidity, or water splashes.

2)Meranti has a rich, reddish brown color and a distinctive grain pattern that gives it an attractive, natural appearance.

3)Meranti is a hardwood that is highly resistant to weathering, rotting, and insect damage, making it well suited for outdoor use. It can withstand exposure to the elements for many years with minimal maintenance
