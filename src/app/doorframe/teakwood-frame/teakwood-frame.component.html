Origin

Teak mainly originates in Eastern and South Eastern Asia , with some species of teak growing in Africa and the Caribbean. Teak trees grow up to 50 metres high and survive for around 100 years, with a tree being fully matured as early as 25, making it one of the fastest growing hardwoods
